Paul Quillen

About

Paul Quillen is a long-time musician living in Eugene, Oregon.

"Quillen's acoustic ballads are dusky little jewels, or the skyline of a city night: reachable but somehow far away. Quillen put out a debut EP this year, and it's terrific. As are his live performances. Eugene doesn't deserve your songs, Paul, but thanks for sharing them anyways."- Eugene Weekly
